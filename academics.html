Academics

Our academic programs are designed to prepare students for the demands of modern industries and research. We offer diverse programs in:

  • Mathematics:
    Developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
  • Kiswahili:
    Fostering linguistic and cultural understanding.
  • Integrated Sciences:
    Exploring the natural world through hands-on experiments.
  • Health Education:
    Promoting healthy lifestyles and well-being.
  • English:
    Enhancing communication and literacy skills.
  • Social Studies:
    Understanding history, geography, and civic responsibility.
  • Agriculture:
    Learning sustainable farming practices.
  • Life Skills:
    Building essential skills for personal and social development.
  • Religious Education:
    Exploring ethical values and spiritual growth.

Our curriculum is aligned with national standards and is regularly updated to reflect the latest advancements in education. We also offer specialized programs for gifted and talented students, as well as support services for students with learning differences.
